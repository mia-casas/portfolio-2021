About

I am a 2020 graduate of Valparaiso University, with a Bachelor of Arts in International Economics and Cultural Affairs and a secondary major in Spanish. I was first presented the opportunity to work with Eleven Fifty Academy in 2018 in a summer program coordinated between Eleven Fifty and Independent Colleges of Indiana. The goal was to introduce liberal arts majors to the wide-range of opportunity present in the technology industry. At the beginning of 2021 I enrolled at Eleven Fifty to experience the complete Web Development course. This site is an evolving display of my skill set in HTML/CSS and JavaScript.

I am eager to take what I have learned and use this knowldege to launch my career. I look forward to working as a freelancer on short-term or long-term projects. To learn more about my skill sets and experiences, feel free to review my resume and reach out so we can partner together.

Static Storefront

For the second Gold Badge Project, students must create a static storefront to present in their portfolio. Students practice creating a wireframe design to guide the layout of their webpage, which consists solely of HTML and CSS. My page features a fictional online retail store for women.

As an introduction to API's, students created a webpage that interacts with the Google Maps API to return data to the user. In this tutorial, the API returns data such as a user's location by generating a custom map, based on the user's location.
